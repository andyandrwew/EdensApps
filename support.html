1. Contact and response times

The primary support channel for Yullah is email:

  • Email: Support@EdensApps.com
  • Typical response time: within 2 business days
  • Hours: Monday to Friday, Eastern Time (ET), excluding U.S. holidays
For faster support, include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

2. In-app purchase support

Common purchase issues

  • Purchased coins/items not appearing.
  • Purchase stuck in pending state.
  • Restore-related issues after reinstalling or changing devices.

What to send us

  • Date and time of purchase.
  • Product purchased (for example, coin pack size).
  • Screenshot of the Apple receipt email or purchase history entry.
  • Any error message shown in-app.

Billing and refunds for App Store purchases are managed by Apple. For refund requests, use reportaproblem.apple.com.

3. Ads and tracking support

Yullah may use ads and attribution technologies to measure campaign performance, prevent fraud, and improve gameplay operations.

  • If ads are not loading or repeating too frequently, contact support with screenshots and your device details.
  • If you want to manage tracking permissions on iOS, review your device privacy settings for app tracking choices.
  • If you believe an ad is inappropriate, include the time shown and a screenshot where possible.

4. Privacy and data rights support

For privacy questions or data rights requests (such as access, deletion, or correction requests where applicable), contact Support@EdensApps.com.

Privacy Policy: Yullah Privacy Policy

5. Technical troubleshooting

  • Update to the latest app version and latest iOS version available for your device.
  • Fully close and reopen the app, then retry the action.
  • Check network stability (Wi-Fi/cellular).
  • For purchase issues, confirm you are signed in with the same Apple Account used for the purchase.

6. Bug reports and feedback

We welcome feature requests and bug reports. Please include:

  • A short issue title and exact steps to reproduce.
  • Expected behavior and actual behavior.
  • Device model, iOS version, and app version.
  • Relevant screenshots or short video captures.

7. Global support notes
