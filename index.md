## 1. Introduction and scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Eden's Apps LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you play the Yullah app (also referred to as “Yullah Launcher”) on iOS and (in future) Android devices. This Policy applies to all players worldwide, regardless of country of residence.

## 3. Data we collect

### Data you provide

- Information you choose to submit when contacting support, providing feedback, or making in-app purchases.

### Automatically collected device/network/usage data

- Device information (such as device type, operating system, and settings).
- IP address and general region (not precise location, unless required for legal compliance).
- App usage statistics, crash logs, and diagnostics.

### Advertising/attribution identifiers

- Advertising identifiers (IDFA for iOS, AAID for Android, used to display personalized ads and measure ad performance).
- Analytics and attribution data (e.g., installs, ad engagement).

### In-app purchase/payment-related data

- Transaction-related information (purchase receipts, time, amount, and item purchased).
- No payment card or billing details are processed by us—these are handled directly by the relevant app store (e.g., Apple, Google).

## 4. Why we process data (purposes)

- To provide, maintain, and improve the Yullah app and services.
- To personalize your user experience.
- To process in-app purchases and deliver purchased items.
- To show advertisements and measure their effectiveness.
- To analyze app usage and troubleshoot problems.
- To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

## 5. Legal bases (GDPR-style)

- **Contract performance:** To provide and operate the app as agreed in our terms.
- **Legitimate interests:** Running, protecting, and improving the game; fraud prevention; analytics.
- **Consent:** For personalized advertising and marketing analytics (where required by law).
- **Legal obligation:** To meet legal, regulatory, or compliance requirements.

## 6. Sharing/disclosure categories

- **Service providers** (cloud hosting, analytics, support software)
- **Advertising/analytics partners** (to show ads and measure their performance)
- **Platform/payment providers** (e.g., Apple, Google) for transactions, account management, or support
- **Legal authorities** (if required by law, court order, or legal process)

We do not sell your personal data. We take steps to require third parties to handle data only as instructed by us and as necessary to provide their services.

## 7. International data transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards under applicable law (such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 8. Data retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Once no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized.

## 9. User rights

### EEA/UK rights

If you reside in the European Economic Area or the UK, you have rights to access, correct, or delete your data; object to or restrict processing; and, where applicable, data portability and withdraw consent for certain processing.

### California/US state privacy rights

If you are a California resident or reside in another US state with privacy laws, you may have rights to know, access, delete, or opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” (for cross-context behavioral advertising) of your personal information under laws like the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: Support@EdensApps.com

## 10. Children’s privacy

Yullah is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13 (or older if required by law in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate parental consent.

## 11. Security

We apply technical and organizational measures designed to protect your data from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access. However, no security method is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
